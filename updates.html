Updates


Initial Plan



15/10/2020

Launch Meeting

The team met for the first time, and the team's Discord, Github organisation and Google Drive were set up.

The game engine was discussed and the team decided to look into 'libgdx' in more detail.

Requirements were discussed in the team and will be considered by the team before the next meeting



19/10/2020

Customer Meeting 1

The team met with the customer to discuss our early thoughts on the game requirements

A Number of gameplay ideas were discusses with the customer, including how neutral NPC characters should be considered

The team also discussed a top-down view of the game, and to start a prototype for the next customer meeting



22/10/2020

The team discussed the progress of the prototype and requirements documentation

The Architecture design was discussed by the team, and documentation was started



26/10/2020

Customer Meeting 2

The customer was shown our prototype and feedback was requested

  • Game looks and feels right
  • Character needs work
  • Scaling of the map needs work

Documentation style was discussed and established



29/10/2020

The Team finalised the requirements for the project, and discussed Architecture design

Planning & Method selection were discussed and the Agile methodology was selected - Documentation to follow



5/11/2020

The team was updated on the progress of the implementation of the game and relevant documentation elements



12/11/2020

The final sprint jobs were discussed and deligated

Implementation set a deadline for merging each working branch into a version of the game



23/11/2020

The team reviewed the state of the game and any features not implemented
